Hamel´s favourite bands
  • Snarky Puppy

    • Snarky Puppy has a very unique style of music, mixing Jazz, Rock, Pop and World Music with ease, the 13 member band has an incredible chemistry and every member truly adds something new to the table, making music not only rich in theory and technique but catchy and danceable

  • CHVRCHES

    • CHVRCHES seems like a band that has been taken straight from the 80´s, with a heavy use of sytnhs and a captivating female voice, their music is incredibly fun to listen to in it´s catchy beats and repetitive drums, they are truly an energetic band

  • Death Grips

    • This trio is taking Hip-Hop to the next level, mixing it with Punk, Electronic Music, being extremely varied but still being so clearly Death Grips, due to it´s extreme noisiness and heaviness Death Grips is not an easy band to get into, but it´s definetely worth it
